Brief Notes

  • window.x === x, but it is easier to just use x
  • .confirm creates a pop-up that asks if you want to proceed.
  • .confirm, .alert, and .prompt are not recommended because they can be blocked.
  • Cookies are small files that are saved locally.
  • document.cookies shows what is in the cookie.
  • data- can be used to embed data with custom attributes.
  • APIs let us pull in information.
  • Local storage lets us store data even when the window is closed.
